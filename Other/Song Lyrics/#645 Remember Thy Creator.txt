 "Remember thy Creator in the days of thy youth," 
 Jesus calls thee in life's morning 
 Through His own Word of truth; 
 Come and trust Him, Boldly own Him, 
 Serve the Lord faithfully; 
 He'll be with you, satisfy you, 
 Till in heaven His face you'll see!
 
 Author: Alfred P. Gibbs 
 Composer: Alfred P. Gibbs
